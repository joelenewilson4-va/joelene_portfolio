About

01 — Profile

I'm Jo — an administrative professional who also runs digital marketing and content for grassroots organisations. I bring the same discipline to an organised system that I bring to a social media calendar: nothing gets missed.

I'm a detail-driven administrator with hands-on experience across typing, document preparation, filing, and general office support — paired with several years of self-taught digital marketing and content writing, built entirely on a zero-budget, results-first basis.

I run ChessChat, a community storytelling site and social presence built around the tagline "Every Player Has a Story," and I manage promotion and content for two chess organisations. That work has sharpened exactly the skills a busy office or remote team needs: organising information clearly, writing copy that lands, keeping platforms consistent, and following through without being chased.

I'm now looking to bring that same reliability to a remote administrative, marketing, or content support role.
